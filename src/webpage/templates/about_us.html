Project Description

This project focuses on developing a medical imaging application that reconstructs and compares 3D volumetric data from video recordings of medical scans. By leveraging computer vision techniques, the application extracts and processes slices of medical imaging data to create accurate 3D models. It also enables users to compare two medical volumes to identify differences, providing a user-friendly and accessible tool for medical imaging analysis. Designed as a student project, this application demonstrates innovation in bridging technology and healthcare.
