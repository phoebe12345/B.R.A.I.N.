What is B.R.A.I.N

B.R.A.I.N stands for Building Reconstructed Anatomical Images for Navigation and is an application that allows you to upload a video captured on your mobile device and effortlessly visualize a 3D model while comparing significant differences. It is designed to assist clinicians and researchers in creating 3D models of the brain, offering valuable insights for those in the field. Let's dive in and explore how this app enhances data visualization with exceptional clarity and precision.
